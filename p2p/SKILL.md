## Core Philosophy

P2P is built on **direct response copywriting** -- every piece of copy must drive a measurable action. Not brand awareness, not creativity for its own sake.

**The 3 pillars:**
1. **Simplicity** -- Write for 10-year-olds. If you have to think about the message, it's bad.
2. **Proven Frameworks** -- Follow structures that work. Originality = unknown variables = risk.
3. **Deep Problem Understanding** -- Understand your reader better than they understand themselves.

**The Theory of Dualities:** Duality creates conflict --> Conflict creates movement --> Movement breeds decision. Every great piece of copy contains a duality.

## Quick Reference: The 6-Step Formula

The primary writing structure for any format:

1. **Verify** -- Start with something they've experienced (gets through self-sealing logic bubble)
2. **Validate Internal Problem** -- Address how they feel (emotional connection)
3. **Pivot to Philosophical Problem** -- Introduce duality/conflict (triggers internal stories)
4. **Explanation** -- Logical reason why (justifies emotional decision)
5. **Benefit** -- Make it irresistible
6. **Call to Action** -- Tell them exactly what to do

## Quick Reference: 5 Basic Elements

Gather these BEFORE writing anything:
1. **Target Customer** -- Specific demographics + behaviors
2. **Desired Outcome** -- Ultra-specific ("lose 3kg of belly fat" not "lose weight")
3. **Benefit** -- Added on top (time, ease: "in under 45 days")
4. **Pain Point** -- Biggest obstacle (handle objections before they arise)
5. **Signature Solution** -- Unique name, avoids category words ("protocol" not "diet")
